About Us

LET'S TRAVEL was founded in Algeria, in partnership with universities and schools
with the aim of simplifying the university application process for future students
wishing to pursue their studies in different parts of the world.

LET'S TRAVEL for students LET'STRAVEL has been specially designed and organized to help students in the best possible way. It allows students to search,
find programs and apply directly through LET'S TRAVEL.
Once you have applied an agent will be available 7 days a week to provide you with assistance.

LET'S TRAVEL for Universities

For decades, universities have been losing opportunities to attract the world's most qualified students to apply to their respective institutions due to the complexity of the application process and bureaucracy, LET'S TRAVEL helps you correct this problem.

LET'S TRAVEL Objective The first platform in the world to provide an agent for each student dedicated to taking care of the student's admission and visa procedure.
